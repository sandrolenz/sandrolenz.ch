The game

The game is about a dwarf mining his way through the earth in hopes of finding gold and other valuables. To make the game interesting, there will be enemys that randomly spawn while mining. Once you get killed or the pickaxe breaks, the game will end and you get a score.

The world is randomly generated and each time you play will be a little different.

Concept Art

Before we started working on the game, we created a simple concept to see how the game would look like. Since this is just a very small game, we didn't go into much detail for it.

Menus

To display the games instructions, we created a simple main menu. The main menu is displayed when you open the game. You can then start the game by pressing "Enter" on the keyboard.

When the game ends, the game over menu is displayed. It shows why the game ended and how high your score is. You can then restart the game by pressing "R" on the keyboard.

Instructions

The game is very simple to play. You just need to use WASD or the arrow keys to move around and you can break blocks around you by left-clicking on them.

  • Use WASD or the Arrow Keys to move
  • Try to find as many ores as you can, before the pickaxe breaks
  • But beware of the spiders that hide deep underground

Unfortunately, we were not able to make the spiders visible when you encounter them. If you hit one, the game just plays the sound and you loose 1 health point.

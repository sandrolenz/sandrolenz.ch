The game

This is very simple game where you work as a cook in a restaurant and serve burgers to your customers.

Concept Art

Before we started working on the game, we created a simple concept to see how the game would look like. Since this is just a small game, we didn't go into much detail for it.

Menus

To display the games instructions, I created a simple main menu. The main menu is displayed when you open the game. You can then start the game by pressing "Enter" on the keyboard.

When the day ends, a simple menu is displayed. It shows how many customers you served and the amount of money you have. You can then start the next day by pressing "Enter" or end the game with "ESC".

Instructions

The game is very simple to play. You only have to use your mouse and drag&drop the ingredients for your burger.

  • Once a customer orders a burger, make that burger by dragging the ingredients onto the plate in the right order.
  • When you're done, click the bell to serve the burger.
  • If you work faster, you'll get a higher tip.
  • The game has a defined daylength, if its over, the end screen appears and you can choose to continue with the next day or end the game.
